Cloud Security: Risks in the Sky

Posted addictiveon March 31, 2025

Cloud computing powers everything, but missteps turn it into a hacker’s playground. Misconfigured storage is the biggest culprit.

I reviewed a 2024 breach— an S3 bucket left public exposed 10TB of data. I mimicked it on AWS, forgetting to lock a bucket. A quick Shodan scan found it in hours. That’s how easy it is.

Hardening tips? I enforce IAM roles, enable MFA, and encrypt everything—S3’s server-side encryption is free. Regular audits with tools like CloudSploit catch sloppy configs before they leak.

Cloud security’s a shared responsibility. Providers secure the platform; we secure our data. One lapse, and it’s game over.
